About ThreatAnalyzer AI

What We Do

ThreatAnalyzer AI is an advanced cybersecurity solution that transforms unstructured Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) reports into a structured format for better analysis and decision-making.

How It Works

  • Users provide raw, unstructured cyber threat reports.
  • Our LLM-powered system processes the data, extracting key insights such as ATT&CK Tactics and Techniques.
  • The structured data is stored in a secure database for future reference and analysis.
  • The data can be remotely accessed from anywhere, ensuring flexibility and real-time threat monitoring.

Why It Matters

Helps cybersecurity analysts save time by automating the extraction of key threat information. Enables quick decision-making with structured and easily retrievable data. Supports remote access, making it ideal for distributed security teams.

Our Mission

To simplify cyber threat intelligence by leveraging AI and automation. To provide real-time, structured insights that enhance cyber defense strategies.
